Patterns

Here's an early Javscript application I made to help learn how to manipulate elements of webpage.

Like with so many other projects, I got the idea while I was drinking a cup of coffee. Specifically, and important to this project, a cup of Peet's coffee.

Peet's coffee is served in paper cups decorated with traditional patterns from around the coffee-growing world.

[[[Picture of Peet's coffee cups]]]

(When I was in college I worked at Peet's Coffee, so I've spent a lot of time looking at these cups!)

The patterns I saw on the cups reminded me of ASCII art, which isn't surprising because many of the prints on Peet's coffee cups were made using stitching patterns or stamps. ASCII characters are derived from typewriters, which in some sense are fancy forms of stamps. As such, I think of this project as a sort of digital version of pattern making.
